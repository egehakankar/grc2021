About Graduate Research Conference

Bilkent University Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department and IEEE Bilkent Student Branch welcome you to attend the 31th Graduate Research Conference (GRC)!

Bilkent University Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department and IEEE Bilkent Student Branch welcome you to attend the 31th Graduate Research Conference (GRC) on 20 January 2021 online! The annual GRC is a whole-day activity where graduate students at Bilkent University Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department present their work in a festival of information sharing. The graduate students present their work in online sessions. There will be an award ceremony for the best oral presentations determined by the votes of the faculty members.
